Welcome to your tea break-time in my castle

I have many rooms in my castle, so you will need to open all the doors, but be careful, behind some of them, there are hot cups of tea. It is important not to spill the tea.

How does the game work?

Click on any door and discover if there is a hot cup of tea behind it - your goal is to avoid the hot cups of tea. If you think there is a hot cup of tea behind the door, click on it while you are holding the shift key. In that way, you will put a biscuit on the door, so you will not open it by mistake.

How to win the game?

Open all the doors that do not have a hot cup of tea behind them. If you open a door with a cup of tea behind it, you will lose and need to start all over again.

Good luck
